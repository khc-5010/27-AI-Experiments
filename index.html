Dip Your Toe

Low-lift experiments you could try this semester

01
Learning & Study Modes
All three major platforms now have Socratic tutoring modes that guide instead of giving answers.
🔑 Steal This: Point students to any platform's study mode for your subject. They get Socratic tutoring — free, on-demand, and it won't just hand over answers.

The Principle That Makes It All Work

Context management — the single idea behind every experiment above

✦
Don't Waste Your Context
The single principle behind every experiment: the quality of AI output is directly proportional to the context you provide. Custom instructions, persistent profiles, knowledge files, projects — they all do the same thing: give AI the information it needs to be brilliant instead of generic.
🔑 The Big Steal: Every tool, every experiment, every chatbot in this presentation is ultimately about one thing — giving AI enough context to be useful. Start there, and everything else follows.
